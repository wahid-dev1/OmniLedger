Everything You Need to Run Your Business

Powerful features designed to streamline your operations, from inventory management to financial reporting.

Advanced Inventory Management

Track products with SKU, pricing, categories, and comprehensive batch tracking with expiry dates.

Point of Sale (POS)

Streamlined sales interface with batch deduction (FIFO or manual selection) and intelligent returns management.

Double-Entry Accounting

Full double-entry ledger system with configurable chart of accounts and automatic transaction generation.

CRM & SRM

Complete customer and supplier relationship management with area-based categorization and history tracking.

Custom Reporting

Dynamic query builder for personalized reports with PDF and Excel export capabilities.

Multi-Database Support

Flexible database configuration - SQLite, PostgreSQL, MySQL, or MSSQL. Switch anytime without data loss.
